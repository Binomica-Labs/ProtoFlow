Title: Transformation and Storage Buffer (TSS) Recipe
Description: A recipe for how to make the chemical transformation buffer for E. coli cells (openwetware.org)
1. To a sterile 50mL centrifuge tube add 5 grams of PEG 3350 (Miralax).
2. Then add either 0.3g of MgCl2*6H2O or 1.5mL of 1M MgCl2.
3. Next add 2.5mL of pure DMSO.
4. Finally, add sterile LB Broth to the 40mL mark, vortex until fully dissolved, and top off to the 50mL mark.
5. Filter sterilize media with a 0.22um syringe filter and store at -20C.
